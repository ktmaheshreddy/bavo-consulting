About us

Our Story

Bavo Consulting Inc. was started in 2022 by Ms. Izabella Aliyeva with over 10 years experience in the business industry. Bavo Consulting Inc. has an opportunity to help businesses in the U.S. and Kazakhstan collaborate towards completing mutual goals, and at the same way strengthening the bilateral relations between these two countries. Ms. Aliyeva will bring extensive knowledge and connections to the table, particularly regarding Kazakhstan's companies, and will be able to help clients search for the best mutual business partners. Moreover, she will leverage her consulting experience to assist businesses in optimizing operations and increasing productivity and profitability, particularly through investments in emerging markets, thereby generating revenue and employment opportunities within the country.

Bavo Consulting Inc. provides market entry consultancy services to help companies evaluate, understand, and seize new market opportunities. The Company brings expertise to market entry processes such as market sizing, competition analysis, supply chain structure, and post-entry growth opportunities. Bavo Consulting Inc. targets companies in the U.S. and Kazakhstan that rely on consulting businesses to avoid pitfalls such as low product or market fit, competition underestimation, and value mismatch offers. The Company already has four clients in the U.S. and one client in Kazakhstan and will continue providing its consulting services to them, as well as search for new clients in these two countries.

Only with trusted advisors and consulting firms, it will be able to build and increase economic growth for the U.S. in the field of business trade relations. Bavo Consulting Inc. will help U.S. companies that engage in international trade with Central Asia, especially Kazakhstan, to be sure of the specific details and obtain important information in the relations with countries in this area. The Company will help companies, both in the U.S. and Kazakhstan, achieve mutually beneficial trade relations.

Bavo Consulting Inc. searches for companies, both in the U.S. and Kazakhstan, that engage in import and export activities between these two countries. Ms. Aliyeva possesses extensive experience working with large companies, as well as with Kazakhstan’s national companies, and significant market knowledge of these companies and their operations. Additionally, the Company works on communication issues between companies and searches for clients in the U.S. and Kazakhstan for mutually beneficial cooperation in the development of the existing business, as well as new projects, assistance in logistics, and more. Bavo Consulting Inc. provides training and education to clients about how to establish and invest in a successful business that engages in international trade between the U.S. and Kazakhstan. Moreover, the Company advises and trains clients’ employees regarding financial matters.
